Mandem United

The Highland Package Store was my first attempt with a real client. Teaming up with fellow friend Scott Dodge, we sought to give the establishment a new image that would cater to both its older and younger demographic. We developed a logo that resembled a droplet, similar to one found from beer, wine, liquor, and any other alcoholic beverage. Along with a new logo we developed a new marketing campaign for the package store, complete with posters, apparel, packaging designs, and a new growler logo.
